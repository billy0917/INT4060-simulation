📐 Exploring Area

Learn how to measure the space inside flat shapes

What is Area? 🤔

Area is the space inside a flat shape. Count the little squares to find it!

Perimeter is the distance around the outside — like a fence around a garden! 🌳

📐 Area = width × height 📏 Perimeter = 2 × (w + h)

📦 Exploring Volume

Learn how to measure the space inside 3D shapes

What is Volume? 🤔

Volume is the space inside a 3D shape. Imagine filling a box with little cubes — count them all!

A cuboid with length 4, width 3, height 2 = 24 cubes inside! 🧊

📦 Cuboid: Volume = l × w × h 🧊 Cube: Volume = side³

📝 What We Learned

Let's look at the important stuff one more time!

📐 Area

📏 Rectangle: width × height
⬛ Square: side × side
🔲 Perimeter: 2 × (width + height)
  • 🟩 Area = the flat space inside a shape
  • 🔵 Perimeter = the distance around the edge
  • 🔢 Count the squares to find the area!

📦 Volume

📦 Cuboid: length × width × height
🧊 Cube: side × side × side
  • 🟪 Volume = the space inside a 3D shape
  • 🔢 Count the cubes to find the volume!
  • 📏 Measured in cubic units

⭐ Remember This!

📐 Area = flat space a 2D shape covers
📦 Volume = space a 3D shape fills
🔢 Count squares or cubes to check your answer
✖️ Double one side → the answer doubles too!
